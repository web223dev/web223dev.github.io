About HAT@Five Corners

Located at the intersection of 102 Avenue, 95 Street and Jasper Avenue, residents of HAT@Five Corners will be in the heart of one of Edmonton’s fastest growing neighbourhoods. With 199 units, renters can choose from one, two and three bedroom apartments to call home. Although HAT@Five Corners is under construction, it is being built with the city’s community-focused mandate in mind and it will be a staple in the Quarters skyline.

About The Quarters District

The Quarters district is one of Edmonton’s most exciting up-and-coming neighbourhoods. This community-focused district provides its residents with banks, restaurants, schools, parks, grocery stores and other amenities. Bordering Edmonton’s downtown, the Quarters district brings everything a little closer to home.
